Homemade Pelmeni: A Delicious Recipe

Cooking pelmeni is a simple process that requires just a few ingredients. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and add 1-2 tablespoons of salt. Carefully add the pelmeni to the pot, making sure not to overcrowd it. Cook the pelmeni for 5-7 minutes, or according to the package instructions. Fresh pelmeni will typically take 5 minutes to cook, while frozen pelmeni may take 7-10 minutes. To check for doneness, cut into one of the pelmeni. If the filling is cooked through and the dough is tender, they are ready. Drain the pelmeni in a colander and return them to the pot. Add a tablespoon of vegetable oil to prevent sticking. Serve the pelmeni hot, with optional toppings such as chopped onion, salt, and pepper.
